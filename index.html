BIOGRAPHY

Luca Procicchiani
Applied IT B.SC. | Developer | Project Manager

Ciao! I am Luca Procicchiani and 23 years old. I am currently looking for work as a developer. My bachelor is from University of Urbino in Applied Information Technology.

I am Italian but I also lived in Hungary for my exchange studies. I speak English fluently and I love to take any chance to practice it more. I love Computer Science and technology in general and I read a lot about these topics. My curiosity is probably my greatest asset since it also gives me the will to learn and grow every day.

I am always looking forward to new challenges, so if you have any… don’t hesitate to contact me!
